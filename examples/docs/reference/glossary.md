# Glossary

Terms used across the Brewlog docs.

- **Dose** — weight of dry coffee, in grams.
- **Ratio** — water weight divided by dose (a 15 g dose with 250 g water is 1:16.7).
- **Bloom** — the initial pour that de-gasses the grounds before the main pour.
- **Channeling** — water finding a fast path through an espresso puck, causing uneven extraction.
- **Immersion** — any method where grounds steep in the full water volume (French press, cupping).
